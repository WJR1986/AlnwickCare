Our Services

Discover our comprehensive range of personalised homecare services in Alnwick and Northumberland.

Companionship Care

Friendly visiting services focused on conversation, sharing hobbies, and helping maintain social connections. We offer companionship to reduce loneliness and promote emotional wellbeing.

Social Interaction Shared Hobbies Community Outings Emotional Support
Meal Preparation

We assist with planning, preparing, and serving simple, tasty meals to support healthy routines — without managing specialised diets or providing feeding assistance.

Meal Planning Fresh Cooking Serving Meals Kitchen Clean-up
Medication Prompting

We offer gentle reminders to take prescribed medication on time, helping clients stay on track with their health routines — without handling or administering any medicines.

Timely Reminders Dosage Awareness Medication Schedules Reassurance
Home Help & Housekeeping

Helping keep the home clean, organised, and safe through light domestic tasks like tidying, washing up, laundry, ironing, and basic household chores.

Light Cleaning Laundry Tidying Up Safety Checks
Shopping & Errands

Support with essential tasks like grocery shopping, picking up prescriptions, posting letters, or paying bills — helping you stay independent and stress-free.

Groceries Pharmacy Pickups Postal Services Bill Payments
Wellbeing Check-ins

Friendly wellbeing visits to check clients are safe, comfortable, and have everything they need — providing reassurance to both them and their families.

Health & Safety Medication Check Supplies Review Family Updates
Pet care support

Assistance with daily pet care, including dog walking, feeding, light grooming, and keeping pets happy and healthy when needed.

Dog Walking Feeding Pets Basic Grooming Pet Sitting
Accompaniment to appointments

Friendly support accompanying clients to appointments such as doctors, hospitals, dentists, or even social outings like hairdresser visits.

Medical Visits Hospital Trips Dentist Appointments Social Outings
Respite for carers

Offering short-term companionship and supervision to loved ones, giving family carers valuable time to rest and recharge — with complete peace of mind.

Supervision Meaningful Companionship Hobby Engagement Peace of Mind
