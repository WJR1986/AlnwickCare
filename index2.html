About Us

Welcome to Alnwick Care, your trusted provider of high-quality homecare services in Alnwick and the surrounding areas. We understand the importance of receiving care in the comfort and familiarity of your own home. Our dedicated team of experienced and compassionate caregivers is committed to providing personalised support that meets your unique needs.

Our mission is to enhance the quality of life for our clients by offering a range of flexible and reliable homecare services. We believe in treating every individual with dignity, respect, and empathy, ensuring they maintain their independence and well-being.

All our caregivers undergo thorough DBS checks to ensure the safety and well-being of our clients.

Our Services

Support and assistance designed for your independence and comfort

Companionship Visits

Friendly visits to chat, share activities, or accompany you on outings and appointments across Alnwick and nearby areas.

Home Help & Housekeeping

Support with everyday household tasks like cleaning, dusting, laundry, and ironing to maintain a tidy, welcoming home.

Meal Preparation

Preparation of simple, nutritious meals tailored to your preferences and dietary needs, helping you maintain a healthy diet at home.

Shopping & Errands

Assistance with essential shopping, collecting prescriptions, and running everyday errands to keep life running smoothly.

Medication Prompting

Friendly reminders to take your medication at the right times, helping you stay on track without managing or administering doses.

Wellbeing Check-ins

Regular short visits to ensure all is well, provide reassurance to families, and help clients feel secure and supported at home.
